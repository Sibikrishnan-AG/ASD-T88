How can I manage stress as a parent of a child with Autism?

• Practice self-care
• Seek support from friends, family, or support groups
• Consider professional counseling

Self-care activities for parents

• Engage in hobbies
• Exercise regularly
• Practice mindfulness
• Ensure you have some personal time each day

Building a Support Network

• Join support groups
• Connect with other parents
• Seek counseling to share experiences and strategies

Strategies for Maintaining Mental Wellbeing

• Prioritize sleep
• Maintain a balanced diet
• Engage in relaxation techniques such as yoga or deep breathing

How can I balance caregiving with personal time?

• Schedule regular personal time
• Set boundaries
• Make use of respite care services
• Support from family and friends
