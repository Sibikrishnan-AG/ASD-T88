Understanding Autism

Autism is a spectrum disorder that affects a person's social interactions, communication, and behavior. It requires a tailored approach for care.

Tips for Supporting a Child with Autism

• Use visual supports
• Establish a routine
• Encourage social interaction
• Be patient and understanding

Communicating with a Child with Autism

• Use clear, simple language
• Be consistent with cues
• Incorporate visual aids
• Encourage non-verbal communication

Building a Support Network

A strong support network is vital. Seek out community resources, join support groups, and connect with professionals who can help.
