Managing ADHD

• Use behavioral therapy
• Maintain a consistent routine
• Provide positive reinforcement
• Encourage physical activity

Tips for Parenting a Child with ADHD

• Use clear instructions
• Break tasks into smaller steps
• Establish a routine
• Reward positive behavior

Importance of Early Intervention

Early intervention can significantly improve developmental outcomes for children with ADHD.

Creating a Structured Environment

• Consistent schedules
• Visual aids
• Clear expectations help children with ADHD feel secure and manage their symptoms better.
