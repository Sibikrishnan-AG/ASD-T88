What is ADHD?

ADHD stands for Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder. It is a neurodevelopmental disorder that affects both children and adults.

Common Symptoms of ADHD in Young Children

• Inattention (easily distracted)
• Hyperactivity (excessive movement)
• Impulsivity (acting without thinking)

Early Signs of ADHD

• Difficulty playing quietly
• Trouble following instructions
• Frequent daydreaming
• Inability to stay seated

ADHD Diagnosis

ADHD is diagnosed through:
• Behavioral assessments
• Parent and teacher reports
• Standardized rating scales
